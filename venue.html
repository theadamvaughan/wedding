Getting There
Four Oaks Farm could not be more central in Sussex terms. Straddling the East Sussex and West Sussex borders, it is easily accessible from all parts of the county.

The Brighton & Hove area is well within sensible striking distance at about 15 miles to the south while the Gatwick and Crawley areas are a similar distance to the north. The Mid Sussex towns of Burgess Hill and Haywards Heath are 3 and 4 miles away, respectively, and both have railway stations on the main London to Brighton line. The main A23 London to Brighton road is a just a few miles to the west of Burgess Hill and M23 and M25 are readily accessible on fairly direct routes.

As previously referred to, there is a wide range of accommodation available locally to suit all tastes and pockets.
